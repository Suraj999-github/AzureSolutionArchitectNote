🌿 ms security, compliance & identity

core concepts + essential details for study
1

security, compliance & identity foundations

🔐 security concepts

CIA triad: Confidentiality, Integrity, Availability. Zero Trust model (verify explicitly, least privilege, assume breach). Defense in depth: physical, identity, perimeter, network, compute, application, data.

⚖️ compliance fundamentals

Regulatory frameworks: GDPR, HIPAA, FedRAMP, ISO 27001. Microsoft compliance offerings: compliance manager, compliance center, audit logs. Shared responsibility in cloud.

🧾 identity basics

Identity as the new perimeter. Authentication vs. authorization. Modern protocols: OAuth 2.0, OpenID Connect, SAML. Identity lifecycle management.

📌 key takeaway

· Security, compliance, and identity are intertwined—identity controls access, compliance ensures standards, security protects assets. Microsoft unifies them across Entra, Purview, and Priva.

2

Microsoft Entra identity & access

🌐 Entra ID (Azure AD)

Cloud-based identity and access management. Features: SSO, MFA, Conditional Access, Identity Protection, B2B collaboration, and B2C. Hybrid identity with Azure AD Connect.

🛡️ Entra Identity Governance

Access reviews, entitlement management, privileged identity management (PIM). Lifecycle workflows, automated provisioning.

📋 Entra Permissions Management

Cloud infrastructure entitlement management (CIEM) – discover, monitor, remediate excessive permissions across AWS, Azure, GCP.

🔑 Verified ID & decentralized

Entra Verified ID – verifiable credentials, decentralized identity standards. Build trust without passwords.

📘 exam note

· Microsoft Entra is the product family formerly Azure AD + new services. Conditional policies, risk detections, and identity protection are core for security.

3

Microsoft security solutions defender & sentinel

🛡️ Microsoft Defender XDR

Defender for Endpoint (EDR), Defender for Office 365 (anti-phish), Defender for Identity (identity detection), Defender for Cloud Apps (CASB). Unified incident in Microsoft 365 Defender.

📡 Microsoft Sentinel

Cloud-native SIEM/SOAR. Collect data across enterprise, use KQL to detect threats, automate responses with playbooks (Logic Apps).

☁️ Defender for Cloud

Secure multicloud workloads (CSPM). Recommendations, workload protection, regulatory compliance for Azure, AWS, GCP.

🔒 security posture management

Secure Score, exposure management, attack surface reduction. Integration with Microsoft 365 Defender and external tools.

⚡ integrated defense

· Microsoft’s security stack shares signals across Defender, Sentinel, and Entra for accelerated detection and response (XDR + SIEM).

4

Microsoft Priva & Purview privacy + governance

🥗 Microsoft Priva

Privacy risk management: overexposure, data transfers, minimisation. Subject rights requests automation. Consent management, privacy assessments.

📊 Microsoft Purview

Unified data governance: data map, catalog, data estate insights. Scan on-prem, multicloud, SaaS. Classification and lineage.

🔏 Purview risk & compliance

Information protection (sensitivity labels, DLP), insider risk management, eDiscovery, audit, communication compliance. Formerly Microsoft 365 compliance.

🌱 integrated scenarios

Priva uses Purview’s data classification to detect personal data. Both share a common policy and insight platform.

🌐 data + privacy

· Purview focuses on data governance & compliance; Priva adds automated privacy operations. Together they cover data security, privacy, and regulatory needs.

📘 necessary details — acronyms
  • Zero Trust – never trust, always verify
  • MFA – multi‑factor authentication
  • PIM – privileged identity management
  • XDR – extended detection & response
  • CIEM – cloud infrastructure entitlement mgmt
  • DLP – data loss prevention
  • SIEM/SOAR – security info & event management / orchestration & automation
📝 possible study notes (why it matters)
  • Identity (Entra) controls who gets access — foundation of Zero Trust.
  • Defender solutions stop attacks across endpoints, identity, email, cloud.
  • Sentinel gives bird’s-eye view with KQL hunting.
  • Purview maps sensitive data, essential for GDPR and compliance.
  • Priva operationalises privacy (subject rights requests, risk assessments).
  • Integration: all share signals and policy to reduce complexity.
